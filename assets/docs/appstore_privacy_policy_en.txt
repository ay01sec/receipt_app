The App collects the following information to provide our services:

2. Store Information

Article 7 (Security)

We implement the following measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of collected information:

1. Encrypted communication (TLS/SSL)
2. Access control via Firebase Security Rules
3. Password hashing
4. Regular security audits
