# Subscription Purchase Information

## Pricing Plans

- **Monthly Plan**: ¥500/month (tax included)
- **Yearly Plan**: ¥5,000/year (tax included)

## Free Trial

- New users receive a **3-day free trial**
- If not cancelled during the trial period, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid plan after the trial ends
- If cancelled during the trial period, you will not be charged

## Auto-Renewal

- Subscriptions **automatically renew**
- Unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date, you will be automatically charged for the next period
- Renewal charges will be the same amount as the original purchase

## How to Cancel

To cancel your subscription:

1. Open the "Settings" app on your iPhone
2. Tap your Apple ID at the top of the screen
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "Receipt App"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

**Important**: You must cancel at least 24 hours before your next renewal date.

## Refunds

- Refunds for purchased subscriptions follow the Apple App Store policy
- Generally, refunds are not available for periods already charged
- For special circumstances, please contact Apple Support
